here is a biography of Elyse for her birthday!

Elyse To was born on April 7, 2012. She has an older brother named Nico To and an older sister named Edlyn To.

Elyse attended Steelesview Public School starting from Junior Kindergarten at age 4, then entered the Gifted program there at age 9. It was there at the Gifted program where she met her favourite person to this day, an individual named Ahyoung Kong. Elyse To graduated from Steelesview Public School on Thursday, June 29, 2023; marking the end of an era.

Tuesday, September 5, 2023, was Elyse's first day of grade 6 at the school she attends to this day, Zion Heights Middle School. Her grade 6 class was 6E, where her homeroom teacher was Mrs. Tharmaratnam, then Mr. Vaughan.

Grade 7 was arguably one of Elyse's favourite classes. As stated in a quote by Elyse herself, "we had the best class", referencing the class 7/8A. Her favourite parts of her grade 7 class were: Having great teachers (such as Mrs. Cattell, Mr. Tong, etc.), being in the same class as her favourite individual (Ahyoung Kong), and Mr. Tong's gecko named Dude.

Currently, Elyse is in class 8F at Zion Heights Middle School. Her homeroom teacher is Mrs. Cattell once again, and she is unfortunately separated from her favourite person, Ahyoung Kong. Not to worry, though, she always gets to see Ahyoung in the hallways when walking to her next class. Elyse declared that she enjoys her current class, but nothing could replace the joys of 7/8A.

So, what does the future look like for Ms. To? Well, in the coming fall, Elyse will attend A. Y. Jackson Secondary School, where she will graduate in 2030. It is certain Elyse will achieve great things in the future, partially because of her favourite person (Ahyoung), and partially because of her impressively high intelligence level.
